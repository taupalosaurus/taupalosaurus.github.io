Welcome to my website.

I am a Ph.D. student in Applied Mathematics under the direction of Frédéric Alauzet in the Gamma3 team at INRIA.

My research interests include:

  • Mesh adaptation for unsteady simulations,
  • CFD simulations involving moving geometries (ALE, FSI...),
  • Moving mesh techniques,
  • GPU parallelization.




I am recipient of an Airbus Group Foundation grant.
